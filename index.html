M.S.DHONI

  • FULL NAME:Mahendra Singh Dhoni

  • BORN ON:July 7, 1981

  • HEIGHT:5 ft 9 in (1.75 m)

  • NATIONALITY:Indian

  • ROLE:Wicket-keeper Batsman, Right-handed Batsman, Right-arm medium Bowler

  • RELATION(S):Sakshi Singh Rawat (Spouse),jiva Dhoni (Daughter),Pan Singh (Father),
    Jayanti Gupta (Sister),Narendra Singh Dhoni (Brother)


M.S.Dhoni is the short form for the name Mahendra Singh Dhoni.Dhoni is an Indian born,International Cricket player,who plays for the Indian national team.He is a very successful,right-handed middle-order batsman and wicketkeeper. He has been the Captain of the Indian National Cricket team from 2007 to 2016.Dhoni was born in the North-Eastern Indian state of Bihar in 1981.He was born in a middle class family where his father was a junior manager in a government-owned Engineering company. From his early school days Dhoni was interested in sports.But at that age he did not have any exposure to Cricket.His main interest then was in Football and Badminton.He used to enjoy playing the position of the goalkeeper in the school football team. It was around his tenth standard in school that he started getting noticed as a good wicket-keeper for Cricket. Till this time, he was only playing for Club Cricket and School Cricket teams. It was only after his twelfth standard in 1998, that he was noticed by some Cricketing officials and selected for the Junior State Level Cricket team. Dhoni started his India A Team career in 2003/2004. Since then Dhoni’s destiny and success as a Wicket-keeper- Batsman went on growing steadily. He rose through the ranks and also in the eyes of the Cricketing Board, and his fans very fast through all formats of One Day Internationals [ODIs], Test Cricket, Twenty20 International, First Class Cricket, etc. Dhoni featured in the 100 most influential people’s list of TIME magazine in 2011.He is also the recipient of the Padma Shri and Padma Bhushan,Indian Civilian Awards.He is an inspiration to all the sports-loving youth of India.


VIRAT KOHALI

  • FULL NAME:Virat Kohli

  • BORN ON:November 5, 1988

  • HEIGHT:5 ft 9 in (175 cm)

  • NATIONALITY:India

  • ROLE:Indian Captain, Right handed batsman, Right-arm medium bowler

  • RELATION(S):Anushka Sharma (Wife), Saroj Kohli (Mother)


Virat Kohli started his International cricketing career in the year 2008, and the ten years that he has played,he has made sure that every cricketing fan becomes a Virat Kohli fan. He has played 71 tests, 211 ODIs, and 62 T20Is in 10 years. He has made a lot of records in the ten years and here are a few of his records – He is the fastest Indian to reach to 1000,5000,6000 and 7000 runs in ODIS and the fastest in the world to score 8000 and 9000 runs in ODIs. He is the fastest in the world to score 30 centuries in ODIs and fastest in the world to score 35 centuries in ODIs as well.He is the fastest to score 15000 international runs, and he is the only cricketer in the world who has 50+ average in all three formats of cricket. He is the fastest player to score 17000 runs in the least number of Innings. He also has the record to score the greatest number of runs as an Indian cricketer in seven calendar years in ODIs, three calendar years in tests as well. And he also has scored maximum runs in the world in ODIS in two calendar years. He is the fitness icon for the youth, and we all know how bulky Virat Kohli initially was. But later on, he worked on his fitness very well and attained a fitness by which he is called the fittest cricketer of India. He is a family man, and it is very noticeable from the fact that he loves his mother, brother, and his wife very much and he likes to spend his free time with family.


ROHIT SHARMA

  • FULL NAME:Rohit Gurunath Sharma

  • BORN ON:April 30, 1987

  • HEIGHT:5 ft 10 in (1.78 m)

  • NATIONALITY:India

  • ROLE:Top-order Batsman/Right-handed, Right-arm off break Bowler

    • RELATION(S):Purnima Sharma (Mother), Gurunath Sharma (Father), Vishal Sharma (Brother), Ritika Sajdeh (Spouse)

Rohit Sharma was born on April 30, 1987, in Maharashtra. He was born to Gurunath Sharma and Purnima Sharma. He speaks fluent Telegu. He came to prominence after a wonderful 2006 U-19 Cricket World Cup. He scored the highest number of runs in that tournament. India lost to Pakistan in the finals. He made his ODI debut for India against Ireland in Belfast. He scored an unbeaten 50 against South Africa in 2007. During his early career, he was a finisher. Rohit Sharma finished a lot of innings with the legendary Sachin Tendulkar. His position was taken by the new upcoming star Virat Kohli. He scored his maiden ODI century against Zimbabwe in 2010. In 2013, he made his permanent place in Indian cricket when he was asked to open the innings with Shikhar Dhawan. Rohit Sharma took India to win the Champions Trophy in 2013. He later created history by hitting 16 sixes in an ODI innings against Australia. He scored 2009 runs off 158 balls in that match. He became only the third player after Sachin Tendulkar and Virender Shewag to score a double century in ODI. rohit Sharma was then picked for the test series against West Indies in 2013. He scored a massive 177 on debut. He also followed that up with another ton. On November 2014, he broke Virender Sehwag’s record of 217 with 264 in an ODI. Rohit Sharma became the only player to score two double centuries in ODI’s. He was also the only player in history to score 250+ in One Day Internationals. Rohit Sharma captained Mumbai Indians to an IPL championship.He is said to be a humble guy. His favorite cricket player is Sachin Tendulkar. Sachin is the favorite player of almost the entire Indian squad. He has scored 2 test centuries, 5 ODI centuries and 2 twenty-20 centuries.
